I have a (nasty) habit of changing the CMS on my blog. WordPress, Jekyll, Anchor, Squarespace… you get the idea. Well, I’ve done it again. I’ve been digging the  <a href="https://medium.com/@Medium">publishing additions</a>  to the Medium platform, so I decided to go the hosted rout and make the move.

My domain and all of my posts have been migrated to Medium and redirects should be in place, so you can read everything on EndOnEnd.og via Medium.com, their app, or any flavor of RSS app you choose. (The  <a href="https://endonend.org/feed">RSS feed</a>  address is conveniently the same as it was in Wordpress, so “Hi” RSS readers, if there are any after all these years of switching!) I’m even working on getting things set up in Apple News.

Now I just need to post more often.
