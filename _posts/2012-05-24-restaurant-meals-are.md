<p id="yui_3_17_2_1_1407674114902_406593"><a href="http://makinghealtheasier.org/newabnormal">According to the CDC</a>, the average restaurant meal is four times larger than the 1950s. Soda size is 6x larger. Hamburger size more than 3x larger. French fries more than 2x larger.</p>

<p id="yui_3_17_2_1_1407674114902_406604">The average American adult? 26 lbs larger, while only averaging 1 inch taller. (For adult men, the averages went 5 feet 8 inches and 166 lbs to 5 feet 9.5 inches and 195 lbs. For adult women, the averages went from 5 feet 3 inches and 140 lbs to 5 feet 4 inches and 164 lbs. <a href="http://www.cdc.gov/nchs/fastats/bodymeas.htm">Source</a>.)</p>
