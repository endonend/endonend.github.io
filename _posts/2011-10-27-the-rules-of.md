Love  <a href="http://zenhabits.net/rents/">this post at Zen Habits</a> , especially “you should always break rules” as a parent. So true!

<ol>
<li>Teach kids to be self-sufficient.</li>
<li>Teach older kids to help with the younger kids.</li>
<li>Teach them to solve problems.</li>
<li>Show them how to be passionate.</li>
<li>Play with them outside, and be active.</li>
<li>Don’t overschedule.</li>
<li>Don’t dote.</li>
<li>Dance.</li>
<li>Read with them, and read in front of them.</li>
<li>Be inquisitive.</li>
</ol>
