<blockquote><p>Those of you who often over-commit or feel too scattered may appreciate a new philosophy I'm trying:</p></blockquote>

<blockquote><p>If I'm not saying “HELL YEAH!” about something, then say no.</p></blockquote>

<blockquote><p>Meaning: When deciding whether to commit to something, if I feel anything less than, “Wow! That would be amazing! Absolutely! Hell yeah!” - then my answer is no.</p></blockquote>
