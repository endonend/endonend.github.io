<p id="yui_3_17_2_1_1407674114902_373231">Pinterest is all the rage, but I don't use it. Sure I checked it out, but it didn't stick. Why?</p>

<img src="http://endonend.micro.blog/uploads/2018/e780f57203.jpg" title="gimmebar" alt=""/>

<p id="yui_3_17_2_1_1407674114902_373235">Well, to start, I got an invite before the site really blew up and the discovery part didn't fit my needs. People weren't pinning stuff of interest, so I didn't have any incentive to return to the site. (And it continued to remain irrelevant the handful of times I did return for a brief moment.)</p>

<p id="yui_3_17_2_1_1407674114902_373238">The main reason, though, was <a href="https://gimmebar.com/">Gimme Bar</a>.</p>

<p id="yui_3_17_2_1_1407674114902_373244">Yes, the basic premise of Gimme Bar is similar to Pinterest, but Gimme Bar does more of what I want and less of what I don't:</p>

<ol id="yui_3_17_2_1_1407674114902_373247"><li>You can save entire pages, not just images. (I save site designs for inspiration.)</li></ol>

<ol id="yui_3_17_2_1_1407674114902_373251"><li>You can back up Instagram, Twitter, Delicious, Pinboard. (I use the Instagram backup.)</li></ol>

<ol id="yui_3_17_2_1_1407674114902_373255"><li>You can back up your entire account to <a href="http://db.tt/X7lFZET">Dropbox</a>. (Meaning you can take your data/collections with you!)</li></ol>

<ol id="yui_3_17_2_1_1407674114902_373262"><li>The actually have a <a href="https://jason-dettbarn-ecz8.squarespace.com/end-on-end/2012/04/instagram-business-models-and-expectations">business model</a>: Pro accounts for $24 per year (<a href="http://venturebeat.com/2012/02/07/pinterest-affiliate-links/">Oops, did I forget to mention Pinterest's shady business model?!</a>.)</li></ol>

<ol id="yui_3_17_2_1_1407674114902_373272"><li>There are no follower counts, share counts, follower/following lists, etc -- no reason to <a href="http://marketingland.com/pinterest-were-actively-working-to-fight-spam-8914">game</a><a href="http://allthingsd.com/20120423/pindollars-brings-affiliate-links-back-to-pinterest-but-gives-money-directly-to-pinners/">the</a><a href="http://pinterestingservices.com/">system</a> or strive for social media "fame." (Just collect stuff you like vs. a popularity contest or trying to make money.)</li></ol>

<ol id="yui_3_17_2_1_1407674114902_373283"><li>Discovering new stuff. It's not perfect, as the user base isn't huge, but they do share things that interest me most of the time.</li></ol>

<p id="yui_3_17_2_1_1407674114902_373289">So, if any of this sounds interesting to you, sign up today and look for me! My username is <a href="https://gimmebar.com/loves/endonend">endonend</a>. Feel free to send me your username as well and I'll follow your collections.</p>
