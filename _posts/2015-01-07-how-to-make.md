Leo Babauta has some <a href="http://zenhabits.net/marriage/">great tips on making a marriage work</a>:

<blockquote>
<ol>
    <li>Learn to resolve disputes with the other person’s heart in mind.</li>
    <li>Most disputes and other conversations are about two things: do you care about me, and can I trust you.</li>
    <li>Finances are the biggest source of friction for most couples.</li>
    <li>Sex is another source of friction.</li>
    <li>Don’t have kids early on.</li>
    <li>Be good at being alone separately.</li>
    <li>But make time for each other.</li>
    <li>You will both change.</li>
    <li>Appreciate the person for who they are.</li>
</ol>
</blockquote>

Having been married for almost 13 years now, I wholeheartedly agree with all of these. Click through to read more from Leo!
