Kottke.org <a href="http://kottke.org/13/10/how-to-be-a-man">linked to</a> some serious link-bait in “<a href="http://gselevator.wordpress.com/2013/09/10/how-to-be-a-fking-man-2/">How To Be A F%#king Man…</a>” today, which purports to be the guide to being a “man” in today’s society. Apparently some people think you need an 85-point list to figure it all out nowadays or that things like owning a “handcrafted shotgun” or being a regular at a bar or that “piercings are liabilities in fights” are important AND make you a douche bag man. (Really glad Kottke called this out too.)

How about these “rules”:

<ul>
<li>Be a good person.</li>
<li>Forget everything anyone tells you about what “being a man” is…</li>
<li>Pretty simple.</li>
</ul>

Stereotypes, expectations, and labels are tiring.
