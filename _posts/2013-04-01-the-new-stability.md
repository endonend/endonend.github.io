Robert Kirkman (creator of The Walking Dead and partner at Image Comics) on forging your own career path vs. relying on companies:

<blockquote>They make the rules. A lot of people have fooled themselves into thinking that’s stability but are now realizing that it’s the exact opposite. The real stability is controlling your own career and being in a position to hire yourself, generating ideas that are enough to make you a sustainable income, and also controlling those ideas and your own destiny. That’s the new stability and that’s something people are realizing. I’m very optimistic that it’ll be something that is here to stay.</p></blockquote>
