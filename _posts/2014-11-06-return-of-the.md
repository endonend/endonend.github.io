Blogging is coming back. Many OGs are starting to re-visit the craft, including Gina Trapani who's <a href="http://scribbling.net/2014/10/16/short-form-blogging/">Short-form blogging</a> post has her "new rules for blogging":

<blockquote>
<ul>
    <li>If it’s a paragraph, it’s a post.</li>
    <li>Negotiate a comfort zone on two axes: personal and public</li>
    <li>Traffic is irrelevant. Don’t even measure it.</li>
    <li>Simplify, simplify. No comments.</li>
    <li>Ask for trusted collaborator feedback.</li>
    <li>Have fun. Blogging is not your job.</li>
</ul>
</blockquote>

I am in... which means this site will likely start to include subjects you aren't used to seeing here. I hope you enjoy the ride, but in the end this isn't my job and it's <em>my</em> personal site. (And yes, I am back to WordPress.)
