Brain Pickings recently posted a <a href="http://www.brainpickings.org/2014/07/07/buddhist-economics-schumacher/">great overview of Buddhist economics vs. Western economics</a> from the perspective of E.F. Schumacher's book, <em>Small is Beautiful</em> :

<blockquote>The former, in short, tries to maximize human satisfactions by the optimal pattern of consumption, while the latter tries to maximize consumption by the optimal pattern of productive effort.</blockquote>

Definitely want to pick up this book and read more.
