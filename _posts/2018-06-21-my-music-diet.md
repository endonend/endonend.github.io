Here are five of my favorite albums from the last month or so:

<strong>Yuno - Moodie EP (Sub Pop Records)</strong>
From his Apple Music profile: "Yuno blends surf pop, synth pop, and R&amp;B into smooth yet distinctive songs."

Translation: catchy summer jams. So good -- hope there is more on the way soon.

<strong>Snail Mail - Lush (Matador Records)</strong>
The debut full-length from Lindsey Jordan is SO good. Can't get enough. Highlights are Pristine and Heat Wave.

<strong>Soccer Mommy - Clean (Fat Possum Records)</strong>
Best description I have is a combination of Liz Phair, Snail Mail, and Japanese Breakfast -- which means awesome in my book.

<iframe allow="autoplay *; encrypted-media *;" frameborder="0" height="450" sandbox="allow-forms allow-popups allow-same-origin allow-scripts allow-top-navigation-by-user-activation" src="https://embed.music.apple.com/us/album/clean/1324319630?app=music" width="660"></iframe>

<strong>Joey Bada$$ - 1999</strong>
The re-released version of his 2012 mixtape &amp; debut. So talented and fresh, even 6 years later.

<iframe allow="autoplay *; encrypted-media *;" frameborder="0" height="450" sandbox="allow-forms allow-popups allow-same-origin allow-scripts allow-top-navigation-by-user-activation" src="https://embed.music.apple.com/us/album/1999/1396290965?app=music" width="660"></iframe>

<strong>The Carters - Everything is Love</strong>
A surprise release from the super duo. Bey shines here for me, but Jay is no slouch. All you would expect from them and more. Check it!
