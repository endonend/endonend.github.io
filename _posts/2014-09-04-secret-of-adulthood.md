Gretchen Rubin's <a href="https://www.gretchenrubin.com/happiness_project/2014/09/secret-of-adulthood-dont-believe-everything-you-think/">Happiness Project post</a> hits the nail on the head. The title says it all.

The absolute, #1 secret to adulthood... to not becomming complacent... to continually growing as a person... and just not becoming a dick, is <strong>Don't believe everything you think</strong>. (Related: don't <em>say</em> everything you think.)

Somewhere on the path to becomming a grown-up we put a lot less emphasis on trying new things, being curious, and learning. I guess those are the choices you make when you are "busy", but they are also a crucial ingredient in figuring out what is actually worth doing.
