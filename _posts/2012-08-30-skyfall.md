<p id="yui_3_17_2_1_1407674114902_400087">A new teaser trailer came out today for <a href="http://www.skyfall-movie.com/site/">Skyfall</a>, the next Bond movie coming this November:</p>

<iframe allowfullscreen="" src="http://www.youtube.com/embed/xJ4dAY3DW4c?wmode=opaque" width="560" data-embed="true" frameborder="0" id="yui_3_17_2_1_1407674114902_400048" height="315"></iframe>

<p id="yui_3_17_2_1_1407674114902_400104">Looks good, or at least much better than Quantum of Solace. I really liked Craig in Casino Royale, but the last one was pretty bad, even considering that it was shot and partially re-written during the writer's strike of 2007-2008. The story was just not very coherent.</p>

<p id="yui_3_17_2_1_1407674114902_400113">Skyfall, on the other hand, has the right ingredients at least: Craig is back, Sam Mendes (American Beauty, Road to Perdition, Jarhead, Revolutionary Road, and a favorite of mine: Away We Go) is directing, and Javier Bardem is the villain.</p>

<p id="yui_3_17_2_1_1407674114902_400122">Can't wait to see the next trailer.</p>
