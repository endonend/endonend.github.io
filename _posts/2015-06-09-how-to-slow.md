<a href="http://www.becomingminimalist.com/more-family/">Becoming Minimalist</a> has some great advice for slowing down as a family:

<blockquote>
  <ol>
  <li>Discern&nbsp;where the motivation is coming from: you or your child.</li>
  <li>Determine an ideal baseline number of activities.</li>
  <li>Elevate the importance of family dinnertime.</li>
  <li>Schedule time for yourself to be with them.</li>
  <li>Leave space between events.</li>
  <li>Develop a family, weekly routine.</li>
  <li>Reduce your personal commitments.</li>
  <li>Own less stuff.&nbsp;It is difficult to recognize how much time and energy our possessions take from us.</li>
  <li>Leave room to add.</li>
  </ol>
</blockquote>
