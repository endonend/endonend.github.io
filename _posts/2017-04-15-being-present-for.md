<a href="https://chrisguillebeau.com/japanese-tradition/">Chris Guillebeau shared</a> a Japanese tradition that encourages us to be present and focus on every experience:

<blockquote>We may interact with the same people, within similar circumstances, but ichi-go ichi-e says that each interaction is an experience all unto itself, never to be re-created perfectly.</blockquote>
